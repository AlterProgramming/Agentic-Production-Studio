# Package: Interactive Story Prototype

## Buyer

An educational, training, museum, cultural, branded-interactive, or technical-visualization team needing a stakeholder-reviewable proof.

## Default deliverables

- One 30–90-second scenario or one bounded interaction
- One primary environment
- Two or three visual or decision states
- Characters, objects, labels, and feedback cues
- Transition, impact, and ambient effects
- Clickable prototype or high-quality interaction capture
- Source and rights register
- State-to-purpose manifest
- Technical handoff notes

## Required client inputs

- Narrative, learning objective, or campaign objective
- Target audience
- Source references and rights status
- Required facts or procedural sequence
- Stakeholder and subject-matter reviewer
- Intended final platform

## Acceptance tests

- A reviewer understands the scenario without a production meeting
- Every visual state maps to an approved purpose
- Facts and technical sequences are reviewer-approved
- Source references are traceable
- Package can be handed to the final development team
