# Package: Mascot Motion Expansion Pack

## Buyer

A brand, social team, or motion agency with an approved mascot, illustrated character, or campaign visual system.

## Default deliverables

- 6–12 short loops
- 12–25 expressions, poses, or reaction states
- 3–6 transparent effect overlays
- Social exports in agreed aspect ratios
- GIF, MP4, WebM, and PNG-sequence outputs as required
- Layered editable source files
- Reusable motion rules
- Asset and export manifest

## Required client inputs

- Approved master artwork
- Brand guide
- Approved palette and typography
- Platform and duration list
- Usage context for each loop
- Required file-size limits

## Scope limits

- Existing mascot only
- No full character redesign
- Maximum agreed animation duration per loop
- One consolidated revision round

## Acceptance tests

- Character remains on-model across frames
- Loops close cleanly
- Transparent exports do not halo
- Crops preserve readability
- Every requested platform format is present
- Editable source maps clearly to every final export
