# Package: Static-to-Motion Product Batch

## Buyer

A DTC brand, marketplace seller, or performance agency with approved product stills and a need for many short-form motion variants.

## Default deliverables

- Up to 10 approved source images
- 15–30 motion variants
- Product isolation and cleanup
- Shadows, glows, particles, labels, and callout effects
- Agreed 1:1, 4:5, 9:16, or 16:9 crops
- Multiple duration variants
- Editable compositions
- Export matrix and manifest

## Required client inputs

- High-resolution product stills
- Confirmed usage rights
- Approved claims and copy
- Platform specifications
- Brand guide
- Required calls to action

## Acceptance tests

- Product shape and branding remain accurate
- No invented or distorted product details
- Text is readable within safe areas
- Exports meet platform dimensions and file limits
- Each source image yields the contracted number of usable variants
