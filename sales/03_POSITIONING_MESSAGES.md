# Positioning Messages

## Small game studio

Give us one approved style and one week. We return a bounded asset drop with animation, VFX, source layers, engine-ready files, and a complete manifest.

## Godot team

We do not hand over loose PNGs. We deliver a tested Godot package with SpriteFrames, pivots, import settings, previews, sources, and QA evidence.

## Motion or brand agency

Your creative team retains authorship and direction. We expand one approved mascot or style into a fully versioned motion package in five production days.

## E-commerce team

Send approved product stills. Receive a controlled motion library with editable effects, platform variants, accurate product representation, and traceable source mapping.

## Learning or exhibit agency

We turn one approved objective or story into a stakeholder-reviewable visual prototype, with every state, source, and handoff asset documented.

## Creative-AI team

Before committing your production team to a model, we run a reproducible bakeoff against your real style, cost, latency, and consistency requirements.
