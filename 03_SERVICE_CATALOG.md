# Service Catalog

## Launch packages

### 1. LiveOps Asset Drop
For mobile or small game studios with an existing visual style and a recurring content calendar.

**Default scope:** 4–8 assets, 2–4 animation loops, 3–6 VFX overlays, source files, engine exports, manifest, QA.

**Pilot range:** $4,000–$12,000.

### 2. Godot Vertical-Slice Art Pack
For Godot teams approaching a demo, funding milestone, festival, launch, or publisher review.

**Default scope:** one encounter or environment slice, one primary animated subject, effects, SpriteFrames, imports, test scene, manifest.

**Pilot range:** $3,000–$8,000.

### 3. Mascot Motion Expansion Pack
For brands and agencies with an approved mascot or illustrated character.

**Default scope:** 6–12 loops, 12–25 expressions or poses, transparent overlays, social exports, source files, reusable motion rules.

**Pilot range:** $4,000–$15,000.

### 4. Static-to-Motion Product Batch
For e-commerce and performance-creative teams with existing product photography.

**Default scope:** 10 source images, 15–30 motion variants, platform crops, editable compositions, export matrix, manifest.

**Pilot range:** $2,500–$6,000.

### 5. Interactive Story Prototype
For education, training, museums, cultural projects, and branded interactives.

**Default scope:** one 30–90-second scenario or one reviewable interaction, layered media, visual states, transitions, effects, prototype capture, source register.

**Pilot range:** $7,500–$15,000.

### 6. Creative Model Evaluation Pack
For creative teams choosing or configuring image, animation, or multimodal models.

**Default scope:** 3–5 models, controlled test suite, quality rubric, latency and cost comparison, one adapter experiment, reproducible deployment recommendation.

**Pilot range:** $7,500–$15,000.

### 7. White-Label Production Sprint
For agencies and co-development partners with temporary specialist demand.

**Default scope:** negotiated weekly capacity, daily review drops, pipeline compliance, source and runtime delivery, QA and manifest.

**Pilot range:** $6,000–$20,000.

## Entry products

### Asset Pipeline Audit
Review an existing asset folder, animation workflow, or Godot import setup and deliver a defect list, normalized structure, and improvement plan.

**Price:** $750–$2,000.

### Packageability Workshop
Convert an open-ended creative request into a bounded package with inputs, outputs, acceptance tests, schedule, and price.

**Price:** $1,000–$2,500.

### Style and Motion Proof
Create one representative asset and one animation or effect to validate fit before a full sprint.

**Price:** $1,500–$3,500.

## Retainers

### Monthly Content Drop
A recurring version of the LiveOps, mascot, or product-animation package.

**Working range:** $6,000–$20,000 per month.

### Reserved Production Capacity
A fixed number of studio days reserved for an agency or co-development partner.

**Working range:** $8,000–$25,000 per month depending on response time and capacity.
